Abiogenesis: Solar Isotoma

Elemental Skill



Arataki Itto creates a Solar Isotoma using alchemy, which deals AoE Geo DMG on appearance.

Solar Isotoma
has the following properties:
    When opponents within the Solar Isotoma field take DMG, the Solar Isotoma will generate Transient Blossoms which deal AoE Geo DMG. DMG dealt scales off Arataki Itto's DEF.
    Transient Blossoms can only be generated once every 2s.
    When a character is located at the locus of the Solar Isotoma, the Solar Isotoma will accumulate Geo power to form a crystallized platform that lifts the character up to a certain height. Only one crystallized platform can exist at a time.
    Solar Isotoma is considered a Geo construct. Only one Solar Isotoma created by Arataki Itto himself can exist at a time.
